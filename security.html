Privacy by Design

Your plans are your business.

At Seekr, we believe travel planning is deeply personal. Our architecture is built to ensure your data is processed locally whenever possible and stays private always.

📱

80% On-Device

Most of your messiest thoughts are handled right on your iPhone. Our local intent detection and geographic gatekeepers verify locations using Apple's on-device maps database—meaning 80% of your requests never even touch a server.

☁️

20% Private Cloud

For complex itineraries or ambiguous requests, Seekr uses a private bridge to Gemini Flash. This is only triggered with your explicit consent and uses a secure, minimal JSON pipeline that doesn't train models on your data.

Smart Bridge Architecture

The "Smart Bridge" uses a three-tier intelligent routing system to ensure maximum privacy and efficiency:

  • 1

    Local Intent Detection

    Extracts duration, activities, and participants locally before deciding if cloud assistance is required.

  • 2

    Geographic Gatekeeper

    Uses the on-device CLGeocoder to verify locations instantly. If verified locally, the process ends there—instant and free of external data transfer.

  • 3

    Consented Resolution

    Only when location is unverified or a generative itinerary is requested does Seekr proceed to our private Google API for ambiguity resolution.

Our Data Promise

Zero Data Selling

We don't sell your plans, your locations, or your group habits to advertisers. Period.

iCloud Encryption

Your trip data is synced via Apple's secure iCloud infrastructure, keeping it encrypted and available across all your devices.
