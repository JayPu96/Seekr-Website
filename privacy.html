Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Seekr ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your Personally Identifiable Information (PII) when you use our app. Please read it carefully to understand our practices.

What Personal Information Do We Collect?

When you register or use Seekr, we may ask you to provide your first name, last name, and email address. This information is used to personalise your experience and enable core app features such as trip coordination and social connection with other travellers.

When Do We Collect Information?

We collect information from you when you register an account, create or join a trip, or otherwise enter information into the app.

How Do We Use Your Information?

  • Personalise your experience and provide relevant trip suggestions.
  • Send periodic emails regarding your use of our Services or announcements about new features.
  • Enable social features such as sharing trips and coordinating with other users.

How Do We Protect Your Information?

Your personal information is stored behind secured networks and is only accessible by a limited number of persons who have special access rights and are required to keep the information confidential. All sensitive information is encrypted via Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology.

Data Breach & Redress

In the event of a data breach, we will notify affected users via email within 7 business days and via in-app notification within 7 business days. We adhere to the Individual Redress Principle, ensuring users have enforceable rights against data processors.
