The Genesis

Start before it's booked.

Most travel apps only care about your trip after you’ve bought the flights. Seekr is built for the brainstorm.

  • 💡 Choose Your Priority: Create a trip as an Idea, move it to Planning as it gains momentum, or let Seekr automatically mark it as Booked.
  • 🤝 Early Collaboration: Invite participants to develop the itinerary together before a single dollar is spent.
Find Best Dates

End the "When are you free?" texts.

No more spreadsheets. Seekr uses "My Availability" to cross-reference everyone's personal schedules and find the optimal timeframe.

  • 🌤️ Weather & Seasonality: Blends your group's availability with real-time seasonal data for the perfect window.
  • ⏱️ Flexible Routing: Search for specific hard dates, or let Seekr find the best 5-day stretch this winter.
The Hub

All trip activities in one place.

Flights, stays, and activities organized effortlessly so you can focus on enjoying the journey.

  • 🪄 Auto-Magic Timeline: Seekr builds your itinerary automatically as you forward reservation emails.
  • 🗂️ Always Accessible: Keep all your confirmation numbers, addresses, and tickets in one offline-ready place.
Groups. Supercharged.

The ultimate Planning Lobby.

Whether it’s an annual reunion or a trip to Burning Man, Seekr Groups provide a persistent home for your travel crews. Maintain a core circle of Members, and selectively choose who becomes a Participant for specific trips.

⛺️

The Group Vault

A persistent repository of group-owned gear. Track exactly who owns the tents, power banks, and kitchen supplies so you’re always packed.

Seekr Planning Models

Plan at the speed of thought.

Seekr’s Smart Bridge uses a three-tier intelligent routing system to turn messy thoughts into perfect plans instantly.

  • ⚡ QuickCapture Bar: Type "Ski trip to Utah" and let local on-device parsing extract details instantly and privately.
  • 🧠 Cloud AI: Let generative models build day-by-day itineraries or build an afternoon around your existing meetings.
🗓️

Your schedule in perfect harmony.

Connect your itinerary directly with your Apple Calendar. View flights, stays, and dinner reservations right alongside your daily meetings and events—all in one organized schedule.

Keep a close eye on your budget in any currency. All participants can log their own contributions, and Seekr calculates the math instantly, making finances effortless for everyone.
